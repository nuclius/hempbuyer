Your Paypal payment is unclaimed?

The seller had an invalid payment email address in the listing.

Please read here:

http://pages.ebay.com/help/pay/claimpayment.html#to

and then share the link with your seller.

If they cannot fix the problem, ask the seller to call Paypal for their assistance.

Do not agree to send payment to a different email address.

You will lose your eBay Money Back Guarantee protection if you do.
